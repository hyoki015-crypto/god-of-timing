HOW TO PLAY

The Goal

GOD OF TIMING is a simple timing game. Your goal is to stop the timer as close as possible to 00.00.

Do not think too much. Feel the rhythm, trust your timing, and press at the right moment.

Basic Controls

Press any key or click the mouse to start and record your timing.

Use SPACE to pause or resume the game. Use R to reset the current run.

Success

If your timing is close enough to 00.00, it counts as a success. Each success increases your combo.

If you miss the timing, your combo resets.

Stages

Each stage has a target combo. Clear the target combo to unlock and move to the next stage.

The higher the stage, the more focus you need.

Just Feel It

This game is not about complicated rules. It is about feeling the moment.

Try to catch the perfect 00. Again, and again, and again.

Good luck.

ABOUT GOD OF TIMING

GOD OF TIMING started from a very simple habit.

Back when I was a student, I used to spend time playing with a digital watch that had a stopwatch function. One day I started trying to stop the timer exactly at 00.00. It was completely meaningless, but somehow it became surprisingly addictive.

The more I played, the more determined I became to hit perfect timing. Instead of paying attention in class, I often found myself staring at the stopwatch and trying over and over again.

Even though it was simple, it felt strangely satisfying. It required focus, patience, rhythm, and timing. Looking back, I honestly think it helped improve my concentration.

Why This Site Exists

Years later, I remembered that experience and wondered: "What if I turned that simple challenge into a small game?"

That idea eventually became GOD OF TIMING.

The goal is simple. Stop the timer as close to perfect timing as possible and build your combo.

How To Enjoy It

There are no rankings. There are no rewards. There is no pressure.

You can play for thirty seconds or thirty minutes. Some people may simply click around for fun. Others may challenge themselves and chase perfect streaks.

Both are completely fine.

Thank You

If you are playing this game casually, I hope it gives you a small moment of fun.

If you are one of the players who takes the challenge seriously and tries to master the timing system, thank you. You are the reason this project feels alive.

No matter how you play, I hope you enjoy the experience.

Good luck, and welcome to GOD OF TIMING.
